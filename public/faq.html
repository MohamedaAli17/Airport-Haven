Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Airport Haven

General Questions

What is Airport Haven?

Airport Haven is your all-in-one airport companion app that helps you navigate airports efficiently. It provides real-time flight updates, restaurant and shop listings, interactive maps, and premium features like lounge access and order-ahead capabilities.

Which airports are supported?

We currently support major airports in the United States, including ATL, LAX, ORD, DFW, DEN, and more. We're continuously expanding our coverage to include more airports globally.

Is the app free to use?

Yes, Airport Haven offers a free version with essential features. We also offer Platinum ($5/month) and Elite ($10/month) plans for users who want access to premium features like ad-free experience, lounge access, and order-ahead capabilities.

Features & Services

How accurate are the flight updates?

Our flight updates are sourced directly from airlines and airport systems in real-time. We refresh the data every few minutes to ensure you have the most current information about your flight status, gate changes, and delays.

How does the order-ahead feature work?

Available to Platinum and Elite members, the order-ahead feature lets you browse menus from participating restaurants, place your order, and pay through the app. Your order will be ready for pickup at your specified time, allowing you to skip the lines.

Can I access airport lounges with the app?

Elite members get exclusive access to participating airport lounges at special rates. You can view lounge availability, amenities, and book access directly through the app.

Account & Billing

How do I upgrade or downgrade my subscription?

You can change your subscription plan at any time through the app settings. Changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle. Downgrades will still give you access to your current tier's features until the end of the current period.

Is there a free trial for premium features?

Yes! We offer a 7-day free trial of our Platinum features. You can cancel anytime during the trial period without being charged.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.
